Home Loans

Owning a home has become one of the most important milestones for India’s rapidly growing middle class. As household incomes continue to rise, so does the aspiration to buy a house. However, property prices have surged significantly over the years—not only in metro cities but also in smaller towns—making it difficult for most individuals to purchase a home solely through savings.

This growing gap between income and property prices has fuelled the demand for home loans across the country. Today, homebuyers have access to a wide range of financing options offered by banks as well as Non-Banking Financial Companies (NBFCs).

A home loan is a secured form of borrowing, where the property being purchased is pledged as collateral to the lender. Because of this security, lenders face lower risk, which typically results in more competitive interest rates compared to unsecured loans like personal loans.

The loan amount you are eligible for depends on several factors, including your income level, credit profile, property location, and repayment capacity. Additionally, applicants have the flexibility to apply jointly with a spouse or other family members, which can improve loan eligibility and approval chances.

Personal Loan

A personal loan is an unsecured form of credit that allows you to borrow funds without pledging any collateral. With simple documentation and easy eligibility criteria, it offers a quick and convenient way to access funds when you need them most.

One of the key advantages of a personal loan is its flexibility. There are no restrictions on how the funds can be used—you can cover expenses such as weddings, travel, home renovation, medical emergencies, or even business expansion.

At Metro Capital, we simplify your borrowing journey by connecting you with a wide network of trusted banks and NBFC partners. Our team ensures a smooth and hassle-free process, helping you select the right lender based on your requirements and guiding you through quick approvals and fast disbursals.

Personal loans are widely preferred, especially among young professionals, due to their speed, minimal paperwork, and versatility. Whether you're consolidating debt, managing planned expenses, or handling urgent financial needs, personal loans offer a reliable and efficient financing solution with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options.

Business Loan

In today’s dynamic and fast-changing market environment, businesses often require quick access to funds to manage operations and seize growth opportunities. A business loan is designed to provide timely financial support, often without the need for collateral, at competitive interest rates.

Whether you are planning to expand operations, invest in new infrastructure, upgrade machinery, or manage day-to-day working capital, a business loan can help address a wide range of financial requirements.

Running a business involves continuous expenses—from initial setup costs to scaling operations and maintaining cash flow. When internal funds are insufficient, businesses can turn to external financing options offered by banks and NBFCs. These funds, known as business loans, are provided with predefined repayment terms and conditions that the borrower agrees to follow.

Business loans offer a practical solution for managing financial gaps, ensuring smoother operations, and supporting long-term growth with structured repayment options and flexible loan features.

Loan Against Property

A Loan Against Property (LAP) allows you to unlock the value of your existing real estate to meet various financial needs. By pledging your residential, commercial, or industrial property as collateral, you can access substantial funds at competitive interest rates.

Since LAP is a secured loan, lenders are able to offer higher loan amounts and relatively lower interest rates compared to unsecured borrowing options. The loan amount is typically determined based on the market value of the property and your repayment capacity.

This type of loan can be used for a wide range of purposes, including business expansion, education, medical expenses, or other personal and professional requirements, without strict end-use restrictions.

The property remains under lien with the lender until the loan is fully repaid as per the agreed terms. Loan Against Property is a practical financing solution for individuals and businesses looking to leverage their assets while continuing to retain ownership.
